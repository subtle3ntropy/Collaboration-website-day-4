Mission For the Homeless

We can end the homelessness crisis. By stabilizing people through shelter, moving them into permanent housing, and implementing assisting programs to keep them in their housing, we can not only reduce, but eliminate, homeless in New York City.

Your donations can help change lives. It is only through generous financial contributions and donated goods and services that it is possible to provide help and hope to the homeless and poor in New York City.

It All Starts With a Meal

We bring hot nutrious meals to those living on the streets of the city, every night of the year. We offer the homeless the training and support they need to find and keep living wage jobs. We give homeless families and single adults the dignity and stability of a home of their own.
